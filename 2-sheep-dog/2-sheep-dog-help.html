Challenge 2: Sheep Dog

Once again, the goal here is to move in a way that makes something else move to the target.
This time it's a herd of sheep.

Differently from a shopping cart, they have a mind on their own. They do move away from you, but in an impredictable manner.
Sometimes they even move completely on their own when you're far away!

- You can only move up, down, right, or left, one cell at a time.
- You can only move once per update()
- Sheep will run away from you if you're closer than 2 cells away. The exact direction they pick tends to be "away from you", but it's randomised.
- Sheep have a 10% chance of moving randomly when youre further than 2 cells away.
- Once a sheep moves onto the target, it is removed from the board.
- The challenge is completed when there are no more sheep.
- Given the size of the board there is now the option to run the challenge at a faster speed.
- state.U is an object exposing a number of utility methods for doing mathematical operations on vectors. You won't necessarily need to use any of them, but a brief documentation can be accessed from the challenge page though the Utils link.
