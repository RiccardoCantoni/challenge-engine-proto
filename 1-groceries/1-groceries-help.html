Challenge 1: Groceries

We start introducing more objects to the environment: a shopping trolley.
The shopping trolley can be pushed by the player by moving into it.

Push the trolley to the target.

Since it can't be pulled, be careful not to push the trolley into the edges of the grid, as it will make the challenge unrecoverable.

- In order to push the trolley, simply move the player into it.
- You can only move up, down, right, or left, one cell at a time.
- You can only move once per update()
- The challenge is completed when trolley is in the same position as target.
- state.U is an object exposing a number of utility methods for doing mathematical operations on vectors. You won't necessarily need to use any of them, but a brief documentation can be accessed from the challenge page though the Utils link.
